🛡️ Privacy Policy
Effective Date: May 4, 2026

1. Data Collection & Processing
The RSS Feeds application is a self-hosted tool. We do not host your data on our servers. All data fetched via RSS feeds, including article content and attachments (PDFs/ZIPs), is stored locally on your own Frappe server instance.

2. Third-Party Services (AI Integration)
If you enable the AI Summarization feature, the app sends article text to third-party LLM providers via the litellm library.

User Responsibility: You are responsible for reviewing the privacy policies of any AI provider (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) you connect to this application.

3. Cookies and Tracking
The application uses standard Frappe Framework session cookies to manage user login. No third-party tracking or advertising scripts are included in the source code.

4. Data Security
As an open-source tool, the security of the data depends on your server configuration. We recommend following Frappe/ERPNext security best practices.
